About

I am Daisy, a graphic designer from New York, proud plant parent, avid DIY’er, cook, and baker.

I aspire to design meaningful and captivating work. I would describe my approach as methodical and intuitive. I see every project with a fresh set of eyes while welcoming every challenge as an opportunity to grow.

Having years of experience as an in-house designer, I’ve had the pleasure of collaborating and learning from many talented people. These experiences have led me to increase my knowledge in various areas including: branding and identity, campaign design, print production, and digital graphics.

I’m available to take on new projects. Please feel free to contact me with inquiries at mandes.daisy@gmail.com.
